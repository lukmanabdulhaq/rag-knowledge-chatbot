# Company Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. To request a refund, contact support@company.com with your order number.

## Eligibility
- Product must be unused or defective
- Request must be made within 30 days of purchase
- Digital products are non-refundable after download

## Process
1. Email support with subject line: REFUND REQUEST
2. Include order number and reason
3. Refunds processed within 5-7 business days back to original payment method

# Shipping Policy

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee.

## International Shipping
We ship to over 50 countries. Customs fees are the responsibility of the buyer.
Delivery times vary by destination, typically 7-14 business days.

# Privacy Policy

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
We use cookies only for session management and analytics.
